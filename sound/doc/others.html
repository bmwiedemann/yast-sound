Descriptions of tools for configurating sound cards.

Configuring sound card under Linux should consist of these steps:

  1. Installing the sound card (hardware).
  2. (Auto)detecting the sound card.
  3. Choosing and installing right drivers.
  4. Creating the device files (if needed).
  5. Testing the installation.
